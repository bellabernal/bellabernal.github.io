What is Onomatopoeia?:

Onomatopoeia is a type of word that represents a certain sound, and is often used for literary effect. By learning various onomatopoeia sounds at a young age, children are able to get a head start in understanding language and its meaning. This learning is important in understanding how letters and syllables sound.
